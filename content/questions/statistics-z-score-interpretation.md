If a data point has a z-score of 2, what does this indicate about its position relative to the mean?

## Options

A. It is 2 units below the mean
B. It is 2 units above the mean
C. It is 2 standard deviations below the mean
D. It is 2 standard deviations above the mean

## Expected answer

D. It is 2 standard deviations above the mean

## Hints

- Z-score measures the number of standard deviations from the mean.
- A positive z-score indicates a value above the mean.
