In the CAP theorem, when a **network partition** occurs, a distributed system is typically forced to trade off between which pair of properties?


## Options

A. Consistency and Availability
B. Latency and Throughput
C. Atomicity and Isolation
D. Durability and Replication

## Expected answer

A. Consistency and Availability

## Hints

- CAP names three properties: Consistency, Availability, and Partition tolerance.
- Partition tolerance is usually assumed; the hard choice during a partition is often between the other two.
